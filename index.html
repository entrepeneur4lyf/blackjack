Blackjack Rules

1. The goal is to get closer to 21 than the dealer without going over.
2. Face cards are worth 10, Aces are worth 1 or 11.
3. Click 'Hit' to get another card, 'Stand' to keep your current hand.
4. 'Double Down' doubles your bet and gives you one more card.
5. 'Split' separates your cards into two hands if they have the same value.
6. Blackjack (an Ace with a 10-value card) pays 3:2.
7. Dealer hits on soft 17 (Ace counted as 11 with total of 17).
